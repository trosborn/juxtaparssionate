<div>Position Description</div>
<div>We take great pride in our software’s quality and test automation infrastructure, and we’re always looking for talented test automation engineers to help us reach the next level.</div>
<div class="paragraph_break"><br></div>
<div>By joining our team, you’ll get the opportunity to work on any of our various projects (e.g., full-blown web apps, REST APIs, client-side apps, desktop apps), with a team of highly skilled quality assurance and test automation experts from around the world. You’ll get to use cutting edge technologies like Cucumber, Watir, Capybara, and PhantomJS every day, and you’ll play a significant role in the development of new product features.</div>
<div class="paragraph_break"><br></div>

<div><strong>Responsibilities:</strong></div>
<div><strong><br></strong></div>
<ul>
<li>Maintain our sky-high quality standards.</li>
<li>Develop automated tests ensuring our products behave as they’re supposed to.</li>
<li>Make strategic decisions about the instruments and techniques involved in the QA process.</li>
<li>Fix existing issues in addition to building new solutions.</li>
<li>Take smart risks and champion new ideas.</li>

</ul>

<li>Experience with automated testing of desktop or mobile apps is a plus.</li>

<li>Be comfortable with iterative and incremental development.</li>
<li>Be willing to help your teammates, share your knowledge with them, and learn from them.</li>
<li>Be open to receiving feedback.</li>
