<div><strong>About the company:</strong></div>
<div> </div>
<div>
<a href="http://customer.io/"><strong>Customer.io</strong></a> is an email marketing platform for web and mobile apps. Our customers include DigitalOcean, Travis CI, Olark, and over 580 others. </div>
<div> </div>
<div>We're building a distributed company. We started in NYC and moved the headquarters to Portland, Oregon. The engineering team you'll be joining is fully distributed. No two people are in the same location. </div>
<div> </div>
<div><strong>The role:</strong></div>
<div> </div>
<div>You’ll work with John, our CTO and Stephen, another Senior Scaling Engineer to scale our infrastructure 100x from where we are today. We’ve already built a service oriented architecture and it’s now time to ensure each piece is able to scale horizontally in a reliable fashion.</div>
<div> </div>
<div>Technologies we use in our infrastructure:</div>
<ul><li>Foundation DB, ElasticSearch, Riak, Go, Ruby, <a href="https://github.com/customerio/fairway">Fairway</a>, MySQL.</li></ul>
<ul><li>We use a mix of Amazon EC2 and bare-metal servers.</li></ul>
<div>We’re currently dealing with &gt; 5 TB of event data stored in Riak and <a href="http://github.com/customerio/esdb">ESDB</a> (our custom event storage system) as well 250 GB of customer data (and growing fast). Over 580 companies are currently relying on customer.io and we want the service to become more reliable for every customer as we continue to grow.</div>
<div> </div>
<div>You’ll be responsible for:</div>
<ul><li>Removing points of failure</li></ul>
<ul><li>Scaling our existing technologies</li></ul>
<ul><li>Replacing technologies that won’t scale</li></ul>
<ul><li>Working on operational issues with the services</li></ul>
<ul><li>Building systems to monitor performance and up time of our services. </li></ul>
