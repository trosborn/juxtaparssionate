<div>We are looking for an enthusiastic Project Manager who believes in agile development. Someone who is goal-oriented and can help keep small and large dev teams on track and focused on sprint goals and milestones. Someone who cares about the people on teams and works hard to find creative solutions so that everyone not only can deliver the work, but can also have fun doing it. Someone who sees themselves as a facilitator on teams and is interested in helping the entire staff continuously grow in their commitment to agile. </div>
